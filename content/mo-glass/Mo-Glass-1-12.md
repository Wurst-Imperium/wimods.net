## Changelog

- Added Turkish translations. ([#53](https://github.com/Wurst-Imperium/Mo-Glass/pull/53))

- Added Indonesian translations. ([#59](https://github.com/Wurst-Imperium/Mo-Glass/pull/59))

- Added Polish translations. ([#62](https://github.com/Wurst-Imperium/Mo-Glass/pull/62))

- Migrated all of Mo Glass's automated tests to the Fabric Client GameTest API. (Fabric 1.21.4 and later versions only)

- Migrated from Yarn to official mappings to prepare for the [removal of obfuscation](https://fabricmc.net/2025/10/31/obfuscation.html) in Minecraft 1.21.11. (Fabric versions only)

- Updated the links in ModMenu.

## Features

- Glass Slabs
- Glass Stairs
- Stained Glass Slabs
- Stained Glass Stairs
- Tinted Glass Slabs
- Tinted Glass Stairs
- Working Transparency
- Working Translucency
