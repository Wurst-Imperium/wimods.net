## Changelog

- Improved a mixin for better compatibility with other zoom mods.

- Migrated all of WI Zoom's automated tests to the Fabric Client GameTest API. (Fabric 1.21.4 and later versions only)

- Migrated from Yarn to official mappings to prepare for the [removal of obfuscation](https://fabricmc.net/2025/10/31/obfuscation.html) in Minecraft 1.21.11. (Fabric versions only)

## Features

- Up to 50x zoom
- No spyglass needed!
- Zoom in and out with the mouse wheel
- No wobbly camera nonsense
- Dynamic mouse sensitivity
- Fully compatible with OptiFine
